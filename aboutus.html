About GeoLocation

This website is designed to help travellers, students, and professionals who are interested in exploring different capital cities around the world plan their trips effectively. It offers a user-friendly interface that is easy to navigate, allowing users to quickly search for capital cities of interest.
Once a user has selected a capital city, the website provides comprehensive information about the city's cost of living, including the price of accommodation, transportation, food etc. The website also provides a breakdown of the average cost of living in different neighbourhoods within the city, allowing users to make informed decisions about where to stay.
